What is AllenOne?

AllenOne is a Python Scraping/Crawler Application developed by Shayon Banerjee, a Computer Science student at the University of Waterloo. AllenOne removes postings from the most popular car trading websites in Canada, such as: kijiji, wheels, carpages, craigslist, and autotrader, and feeds the postings onto a well-designed, easy-to-use website. Each car posting will contain an image of the car being viewed, a link to the actual posting, and a description of the car; including price and kilometer details.

Disruptive

AllenOne was created with the desire to facilitate the search for a used car, by bringing all the postings from the most visited car trading websites in Toronto onto one webpage.

Easy to navigate

With a car in mind and the simple click of a button, you will be redirected to a page containing all of the postings regarding the selected car in Toronto.

WELL DOCUMENTED

The source code for the python crawler and the web design can all be accessed by following the github link on the contact page.
